Create a list of all the tasks you need to complete.

Determine which tasks are most important or time-sensitive.

Group similar tasks together (e.g. errands, work-related, personal).

Set realistic deadlines for each task based on their importance and complexity.

If a task seems overwhelming, break it down into smaller, manageable steps.

Estimate how long each task will take to complete.

Block out time in your calendar to work on each task.

Regularly review your progress and adjust your plans as needed.

Celebrate each completed task to stay motivated and positive.

If you don't complete a task on time, learn from the experience and adjust your approach for next time.
